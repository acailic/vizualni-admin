# Tutorials for Vizualni Admin

## What are tutorials?

Tutorials are interactive, step-by-step guides that help you learn how to use Vizualni Admin to create, customize, and embed visualizations of open data from the Serbian Open Data Portal (data.gov.rs). Each tutorial is designed to be easy to follow, with examples, code, and live demonstrations.

## Who are they for?

Tutorials are for everyone – from complete beginners who have never worked with data visualizations, to experienced developers and data analysts who want to master advanced features. Regardless of your knowledge level, you'll find tutorials that suit your needs. They are available in Serbian and English.

## What can you learn?

Our tutorials cover a wide range of topics:

- **Getting Started**: Create your first visualization in just a few minutes.
- **Chart Types**: Learn when to use bar charts, line charts, pie charts, and others.
- **Embedding Visualizations**: Learn how to embed charts on WordPress, React applications, or any website.
- **Working with API**: Use the data.gov.rs API to download and process data.
- **Advanced Topics**: Custom data transformations, filters, and performance optimization.

Each tutorial has an estimated duration and difficulty level (beginner, intermediate, advanced), so you can choose what suits you best.

## How to get started?

1. Visit the [tutorials page](/tutorials) to browse all available guides.
2. Select a tutorial that interests you and follow the steps.
3. If you need additional help, check the [documentation](/docs) or [demo gallery](/demos).

Tutorials are free and available 24/7. Start today and turn your data into compelling visualizations!
